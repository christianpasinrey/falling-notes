Falling Notes

nine featured pieces · a library of 2124

This library exists because of the Mutopia Project — a quiet guild of volunteers who, since the year 2000, have been engraving the music of centuries note by note, and giving it away to everyone, forever. No paywall, no applause; just the patient conviction that Bach and Satie and a thousand forgotten dance tunes belong to all of us. Libraries are how a civilization remembers. This page only makes their gift visible — the memory itself is theirs.

Thank you, keepers of the score. ♪
